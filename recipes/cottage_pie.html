Cottage Pie

Cottage Pie, made with beef and vegetables

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 carrots, diced
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 ½ cups beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup milk
  • ¼ cup butter, softened
  • ¼ pound shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Combine beef broth and tomato paste together in a jug; add to beef mixture.
  3. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  4. Meanwhile, place diced potatoes in a medium saucepan; cover with water and place over high heat.
  5. Spoon ground beef mixture into a 9x13-inch baking dish; spread mashed potatoes on top and sprinkle with grated Cheddar cheese.
  6. Bake in preheated oven until top is browned and cheese is bubbly, about 25 minutes.
