Tacos

Tacos, made with meat and vegetables

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 tbsp chili Powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¾ tsp cumin
  • Taco Shells
  • ▢¼ tsp garlic powder
  • ¼ tsp onion powder
  • ½ cup tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add the ground beef.
  2. Drain any excess grease from the skillet. Then return to the stove and reduce the heat to low.
  3. Add the 1/2 cup tomato sauce and taco seasoning. Stir together until the meat is coated in the sauce.
  4. Allow to simmer for 5 minutes.
